A short description about myself

I am a passionate software-developer, striving to become an expert in the field. I am also a father of 2 and try to make life with the family as adventurous as possible. So after 2 years of business college, I started on a 13 year trip across Europe and parts of Asia. I have lived in different countries, met many great and inspiring people and am now coming back home to Austria for another adventure. In my time abroad, I have learned the different skills that now make up who I am. Together with my wife and kids I live life to the fullest - every day.


Experience in software-development

My experiences range from hobby-projects to semi-commercial ones. I have worked a few years as an IT support assistant at the Austrian Embassy in Copenhagen. Although I have never worked for a tech company or as a software-developer per se, I have experience in project development and working in small teams.
